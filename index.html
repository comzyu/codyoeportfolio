Issue 001
"I make things. Not just content — things that feel like something."

Arts Management diploma, NAFA, class of 2023. Built real experience at photography studios, restaurant groups, and live creative events — always doing the work, never just talking about it.

Premiere Pro and CapCut for the cut and the rhythm. Photoshop for the composite, the colour, the layer that makes it land. Procreate is where the characters live. The camera is always on. The only thing that matters is whether it actually feels like something when you watch it back.

"The moment when it looks exactly how it should feel — that's the whole reason."

背景 · Background

@six5.crew is where most of this came together. Since 2022, I've been the creative engine behind their brand identity, social content, event posters, merchandise design, and promotional strategy. Seek In Xtra. Over + The Horizon. Concept to print, concept to screen — every time.

Outside of that: Foragers restaurant group (Carrotsticks, Miznon, North Miznon) — content planning, food photography, daily social posting, audience storytelling. One Click Studios — camera operations, Photoshop retouching, client coordination. Visual Arts Centre — event design, video editing, studio operations.

Real briefs. Real audiences. Real deadlines. I'm most useful in the room where the idea is still being figured out.
